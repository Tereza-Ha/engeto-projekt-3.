Upper Austria

Upper Austria, known as Oberösterreich in German, is a captivating region that offers an enticing mix of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and outdoor adventures. Nestled in the northern part of Austria, this region is characterized by its picturesque landscapes, from the rolling hills and dense forests to the stunning Alpine peaks and tranquil lakes. The capital city, Linz, is a vibrant cultural hub with a rich history, modern art installations, and a dynamic music scene, offering a perfect blend of tradition and contemporary flair.

For nature enthusiasts, Upper Austria is a paradise. The Salzkammergut area, a UNESCO World Heritage site, boasts crystal-clear lakes like Wolfgangsee and Hallstätter See, surrounded by majestic mountains. Hiking, cycling, and water sports are popular activities here, providing endless opportunities to explore the great outdoors. In winter, the region transforms into a snowy wonderland, with numerous ski resorts such as Dachstein West offering excellent slopes for skiing and snowboarding.

Cultural experiences abound in Upper Austria, with charming towns like Hallstatt and Bad Ischl that are steeped in history. Hallstatt, often referred to as one of the most beautiful villages in the world, is renowned for its prehistoric salt mines and picturesque setting by the lake. Bad Ischl, once the summer residence of Emperor Franz Joseph I, offers a glimpse into imperial history with its grand villas and the Kaiserpark.

Upper Austria's culinary scene is equally impressive, featuring hearty regional dishes and world-famous pastries. Traditional taverns, known as Gasthäuser, serve delicious meals made from locally sourced ingredients, while charming cafes offer a taste of the region's famous Linzer Torte.

Whether you're seeking outdoor adventures, cultural enrichment, or simply a serene escape, Upper Austria has something to offer every traveler. Its unique blend of natural splendor and rich heritage makes it a destination worth exploring.

Our Tips

Hallstatt: A Timeless Alpine Village

Nestled on the shores of Hallstätter See, Hallstatt is often described as one of the most beautiful villages in the world. Its idyllic setting, with charming houses perched along the lakeside and surrounded by towering mountains, makes it a picture-perfect destination. Venture into the Hallstatt Salt Mine, one of the world's oldest, where guided tours provide fascinating insights into the history of salt mining. For breathtaking views, visit the Skywalk Hallstatt, an observation deck offering panoramic vistas of the village and lake. Enjoy the serene beauty of Hallstätter See by renting a boat or taking a leisurely swim in its crystal-clear waters.

Linz: A Fusion of Tradition and Innovation

As the capital of Upper Austria, Linz is a city that seamlessly blends historical charm with modern innovation. Known for its vibrant cultural scene and cutting-edge technology, Linz offers a diverse array of attractions. The Ars Electronica Center, a unique museum and research facility, focuses on the intersection of technology, art, and society with interactive exhibits and engaging events. Art lovers will appreciate the impressive collection at the Lentos Art Museum, featuring works by Gustav Klimt and Egon Schiele. Take a ride on the historic tram up to Pöstlingberg for stunning views of the city and the Danube River, and visit the baroque church at the top.

Gmunden: The Jewel of Lake Traunsee

Located on the northern shore of Lake Traunsee, Gmunden is a charming town known for its beautiful lakeside setting, historic buildings, and renowned ceramic industry. Traunsee, an expansive and clear lake, is perfect for sailing, fishing, and swimming, surrounded by stunning mountainous landscapes. Visit Schloss Ort, a picturesque castle set on an island in the lake, connected to the mainland by a wooden bridge, and a popular venue for cultural events and weddings. Gmunden is also famous for its traditional hand-painted ceramics; take a tour of the ceramic factory to see artisans at work and browse the shop for unique and beautifully crafted pieces.
